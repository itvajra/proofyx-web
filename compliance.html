Track Compliance and Maintain Audit Readiness

What It Does

Compliance Management provides a structured approach to tracking compliance requirements, implementing controls, and maintaining the evidence needed for audits.

  • Track Requirements and Controls
    Organize compliance requirements by framework (currently PCI DSS, with future frameworks expandable). Map controls to requirements and track implementation status.
  • Clear Pass/Fail Status
    Every control shows a clear status: compliant (green), non-compliant (red), or in progress. No ambiguity about where you stand.
  • Maintain Audit Evidence
    Attach evidence directly to controls. Documents, screenshots, logs, and attestations are stored with timestamps and audit trails.
  • Evidence Integrity
    All evidence is timestamped and linked to the user who uploaded it. Immutable audit logs track all changes to compliance status.
  • Audit-Ready Reports
    Generate compliance reports that show current status, evidence, and history for each requirement. Built for assessors.
