What It Does

Script Integrity Monitoring helps merchants meet PCI DSS 4.0 requirements for monitoring scripts on payment pages. Detect unauthorized changes and maintain audit evidence.

  • Monitor Scripts on Payment Pages
    Scan payment pages to identify all scripts. Track script sources, content, and behavior patterns.
  • Detect Unauthorized Changes
    Alert when scripts change unexpectedly. Identify new scripts, modified scripts, or suspicious behavior patterns.
  • Continuous Visibility
    Automated scanning on a schedule you define. Always know what scripts are running on your payment pages.
  • Logs and Alerts as Audit Evidence
    All scans, detections, and alerts are logged with timestamps. Export logs for PCI DSS assessors.
  • Risk Scoring
    Scripts are analyzed for risky behaviors (form field access, network requests, obfuscation). Clear risk indicators help prioritize investigation.
